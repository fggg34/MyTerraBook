From our base 20 minutes outside Reykjavík, this 2023 Winnebago Revel 4×4 is built for the Ring Road and the highland F-roads beyond it. Lithium house batteries and roof solar mean you can camp off-grid for days — no loud generator to spoil the silence under the northern lights. Inside you'll find a power lift bed, a wet bath with hot shower, an induction cooktop and a 12V fridge. Diesel heating keeps it cosy down to −15°C, so it runs comfortably in every season. We meet you at the door, walk you through every system, and we're a phone call away for the whole trip. Free airport transfer from KEF is included on stays of 5 nights or more.
